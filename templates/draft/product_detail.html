Product Description

Our premium organic moringa powder is made from carefully selected moringa oleifera leaves grown in the fertile soils of Benin. Known as the "Miracle Tree," moringa has been used for centuries in traditional African medicine for its exceptional nutritional profile and health benefits.

This superfood powder contains over 90 nutrients, including 18 amino acids, 36 anti-inflammatory compounds, and high levels of iron, calcium, and vitamins A, C, and E. Our moringa is shade-dried at low temperatures to preserve maximum nutritional value and has a mild, earthy flavor that blends well with smoothies, juices, and food.
