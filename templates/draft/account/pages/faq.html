Store your herbal products in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Here are specific guidelines:

  • Powders: Keep in airtight containers, use within 2 years
  • Oils: Store in dark glass bottles, refrigerate after opening
  • Tisanes: Keep in sealed containers, use within 3 years
  • Fresh herbs: Refrigerate and use within 1 week

Tisanes are herbal infusions made from various parts of plants (leaves, flowers, roots, bark) that are naturally caffeine-free. Unlike regular teas that come from the Camellia sinensis plant, our tisanes are:

  • 100% caffeine-free
  • Made from traditional African herbs
  • Blended for specific wellness benefits
  • Can be enjoyed any time of day
